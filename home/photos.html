Lost Memories

You see a collection of random objects.

First there is a small toy. A small rattle, something that would only interest a young toddler.

The next is a framed photo of a young boy proudly holding a fishing pole with a small catfish. His toothy grin and wild brown hair amuses you.

Another object follows the picture. This time it's a baseball cap. Long since faded with time, it bares no allegance to any team.

The last object you have a hard time focusing on.

This object is another photo frame but when you try to look at it, it gets hazy and your head begins to hurt.
