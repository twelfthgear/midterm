The Frozen Plains

You step out into the open, white plains.

In the distance there is are white capped mountains. You see a narrow mountain passage.

to the left to the mountians there is a pine forest, freshly dusted with a blanket of snow.

A quick scan of the surrounding area shows nothing but barren, white land.

Where your next step may lead you, you are unsure.

You do know one thing for sure. You're not in Kansas anymore.
