The Abandoned Cabin

You turn away from the door to realize the space you had once entered is no more.

Toward the front of this vacant space there is a lone, stainless steel door.

The kitchenette to the far left has disappeared and it's contents have vanished.

The curtains are now tattered and sapped of their once contrasting colors.

Where the roaring fire once sat, a hollow space filled with long cold ashes remains in it's place.

You still feel as if you are being watched in this small space.

You are still slightly unsettled.
