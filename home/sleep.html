The Noise.

A simple, 4 legged futon rests underneath you as you close your eyes to the sound of a crackling fire.

As you begin to lull yourself back to sleep, you hear a terrible shrill sound eminate from below the cabin.

A terrible chill surrounds your body as you lay there paralyzed, filled with fear. After a moment, the sound stops and you are able to move again.

You feel uneasy. Something inside you tells you sleeping is a bad idea.

Whatever made that sound, you are unsure. You know one thing for certain, you shouldn't sleep again anytime soon.
