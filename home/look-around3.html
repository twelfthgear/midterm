The Abandoned Cabin

You turn back to the open space.

The kitchenette to the far left is covered in a thick layer of snow, wind blowing in from the window.

The curtains are now tattered and scattered on the floor, sapped of their once bright colors.

Where the roaring fire burned a hollow space filled with long dead ashes remains in its place.

You still feel as if you are being watched in this small space.

You are still slightly unsettled.
