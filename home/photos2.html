A CURIOUS PHOTO

Sitting alone atop the fireplace mantle is a single photo frame.

As you stare at the photo frame you see a heavily bandaged young man laying in a hospital bed.

He is propped up by various cables, casts, and tubes.

Nearby a few people stand around looking at the camera, smiling and posing around the unconscious young man in support of his recovery.

Whoever this young man is, he sure has some good friends.

And then something clicks. One of the people standing near the hospital bed seems familiar to you.
