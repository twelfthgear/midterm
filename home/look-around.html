The Cabin

You feel lightheaded as you scan the surrounding area.

Toward the front of this vacant space there is a lone, stainless steel door.

To the far left there is a small kitchenette in the corner.

To the right of the door there are a set of red and black curtains.

Immediately behind you a roaring fireplace lights up the space and heats the room.

You feel slightly unsettled as if you are being watched in this small, yet cozy space.
