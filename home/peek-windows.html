Barren Snowstorm

You peer through the tiny slits between the boards.

Your eyes adjust after a second of blinding light.

You see barren land covered in a heavy blanket of snow.

Flurries of frozen rain whip to and fro, seemingly forever.

Far away, there is a treeline, overshadowed by stiff mountainside peaks

Despite being in a warm environment, this too gives you a chill.
