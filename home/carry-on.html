The Frozen Landscape

You crack open the door and are met with a blast of chilling wind. It doesn't bite much else other than your mostly uncovered face.

Before you, a frozen wasteland expands as far as the eye can see.

Visibility isn't terrible, as you can see mountains straight ahead.

To the left of the mountains you see a wooded forest comprised of many freshly snowed on pine trees.

A quick scan of the surrounding land presents nothing but crystalline, white plains
